Try the object replacement backend

Upload a source video and a product image, or paste URLs if you already have them hosted. The backend builds a photorealistic anchor with GPT Image 2, then uses PixVerse Swap to replace the target person, object, or background in the original video.

PixVerse is most economical around 5-second clips, but this backend accepts clips up to 30 seconds for infrastructure testing. Longer clips cost more and may process slower.

Sent to GPT Image 2 to synthesise a photorealistic anchor frame by editing the first frame with the reference image. Describe lighting, reflections, materials, or placement constraints.

PixVerse maps the swap from the selected keyframe at 24 FPS. Use 1 for the first frame, 24 for about 1 second in, 48 for about 2 seconds in, and so on.
